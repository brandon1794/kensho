# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Kensho to be a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. Maintainers and contributors are expected
to interact respectfully and in good faith.

## Our standard

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
as its Code of Conduct. Please read it — it defines the behavior we expect and
the behavior that is not tolerated. By participating (issues, pull requests,
discussions), you agree to uphold it.

In short: be kind, be constructive, assume good intent, and keep discussion
focused on the work. Personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory language
are not welcome.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
maintainers at **conduct@kaizenreports.com**. Reports are handled
confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and ban
participants who violate this Code of Conduct.
